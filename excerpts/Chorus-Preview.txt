Ragaan vich daud di tu agg banke, pure desire
Munda tere pichhe kalla ho gaya jhalla, whisper and fire
Dooriyaan chubh diyan dil nu kach wangu, no escape
Tere bina pall vi na kattda haan main, changing shape
Jad takkda main tenu thand pe jawe, catch my eye
Chhad ditta jag main taan ikk tere karke, watch me fly
Gunaah kar baitha tere naal ni pyaar da, hold me tight
Saari raat jaagda main kalla bas tere layi, through the night

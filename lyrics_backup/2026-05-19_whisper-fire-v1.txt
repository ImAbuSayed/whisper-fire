[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook 1]
Akhiyan de cho painda ae nasha ni, feel the fire
Hanereyan ch marda haan main tera, taking higher
Sikhar te bolda nasha tere pyaar da, mind blowing
Tere layi khada haan nitt kalla, heart glowing

[Hook 2]
Sikhar dupehar jehi teri takkni, burn it down
Oh ni main shonk nal marda haan, own the crown
Bullyan chon dulda ae jadon haasa, make me fly
Raat de saaye ch kalla main labhda, soaring high

[Verse 1]
Kach de glass ch tera parchhawan wajda, moving fast
Saanh ruk jande wekh teri taur nu, built to last
Ajj thehar ja tu thoda mere kol ni, close to me
Tere naina vich dubb ke main jaawan, deep sea
Zehar jehi lagdi ae teri har gal ni, dangerous game
Chup karke sehan main teriyan adawaan, calling my name
Teri har jhalak utte sab kujh vaaran, lose control
Dhuen wangu udd da haan kalla nitt main, empty soul

[Pre-Chorus]
Khaban de vehde ch tere qadam ne, fading light
Parhaayian de wangu tu samajh na aaven, sleepless night
Hawa vich khushbu aa teri hi ghumdi, losing breath
Tenu paun lai kalla main paagal ho janda, life and death

[Chorus]
Ragaan vich daud di tu agg banke, pure desire
Munda tere pichhe kalla ho gaya jhalla, whisper and fire
Dooriyaan chubh diyan dil nu kach wangu, no escape
Tere bina pall vi na kattda haan main, changing shape
Jad takkda main tenu thand pe jawe, catch my eye
Chhad ditta jag main taan ikk tere karke, watch me fly
Gunaah kar baitha tere naal ni pyaar da, hold me tight
Saari raat jaagda main kalla bas tere layi, through the night

[Verse 2]
Tyre'an di aawaz kaale raste te gunje, burning rubber
Dhul udd di hawa ch seene nu lakove, undercover
Dil di machine cheekan ajj maardi ae, revving high
Teri bewafai da zehar main peenda haan, reaching the sky
Wafa da pakka main munda haan kalla, real deal
Kachcheyan de wangu na kade vi bhajda, heart of steel
Khoon vichon jhalakdi aa teri hi tasveer, toxic pain
Dard eh kalla nitt jarda haan main, in the rain
